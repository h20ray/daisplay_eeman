## üìñ Features

Interesting features of Eeman:

- **Quran Offline** : Enjoy the ease of reading Quran anywhere, anytime without internet connection. Read meaningful verses and find serenity through recitation and tadabbur.

- **Murattal Audio** : Play Murattal of each surah by Abdurrahman Al-Ausy (audio source : [everyayah](https://everyayah.com))

- **Daily Prayers** : Find a collection of prayers that you can read and practice every day, helping you deal with situations and strengthen your relationship with God.

- **Prayer Schedule** : Never miss a prayer with accurate prayer time reminders. The prayer schedule will be adjusted according to your location, ensuring you perform your prayers on time.

- **Digital prayer beads** : Use digital prayer beads for remembrance and prayer. Count each prayer beads easily through your mobile phone, strengthening your spiritual bond.

- **Prayer Alarm** : Stay awake at prayer time with customized prayer alarm. Hear the call to prayer or a soothing alarm sound when prayer time arrives.

- **Intuitive User Interface** : Easy navigation and simple interface ensure a comfortable and pleasant user experience.

Eeman is designed for everyone, both beginners and Muslim practitioners who want to live a better Islamic life. Put on Eeman and discover the beauty in your spiritual journey.
